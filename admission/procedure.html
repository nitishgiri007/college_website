Procedure

Minimum ability

Those who have cleared 10th and have secured minimum 35% marks in Science & Math. Eligible Candidate will appear in the PECE Examination conducted by JCECE Board every year normally in month Of May.

Document Required At The Time Of Admission

  • -Photograph : 5(five) copies of colour passport size (4*6)
  • -DLCECE rank card(original) .
  • -AdmitCard of DLCECE(original) .
  • -Seat alloatment certificate from (original) .
  • -Migraion Certificate(original) in case of candidate having passed from a board other than JAC .
  • -Matriculation pass certificate and marksheet/Grade card
  • -Medical fitness certificate obtained from the medical centre authorized by the institute
  • -ST/SC/OBC/PH Certificate(if applicable)
  • -Character certificate in original from the head of the institution last attanded
  • -Income certificate
  • ( Note : All the above documents must be submitted in original for verification at our end.)
